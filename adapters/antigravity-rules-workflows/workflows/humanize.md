# Humanize Text

Description: Remove signs of AI-generated writing.

1. **Analyze** the text for AI patterns (see SKILL.md):
   - Significance inflation ("pivotal moment")
   - Superficial -ing phrases ("showcasing", "highlighting")
   - AI vocabulary ("delve", "tapestry", "nuanced")
   - Chatbot artifacts ("I hope this helps", "Certainly!")

2. **Rewrite** to sound natural:
   - Use simple verbs ("is", "has") instead of "serves as".
   - Be specific (dates, names) instead of vague ("experts say").
   - Add voice/opinion where appropriate.

3. **Output**: The humanized text.
